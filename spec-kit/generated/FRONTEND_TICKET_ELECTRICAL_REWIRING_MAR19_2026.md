# Ticket - Electrical Rewiring

**Date**: March 19, 2026

## Summary
Create a dedicated rewiring ticket for electrical jobs, with clear repair-vs-install distinctions.

## Job Tags
- Jobs: `Jobs`, `Quick Jobs`, `Search`, `Job Posting`
- Skill Tags: `Electrical`, `Rewiring`, `Repair`, `Installation`

## Product Gaps
- Rewiring is buried inside general electrical work.
- Customers need clearer safety and certification cues.
- Repair and new-install flows are not separated strongly enough.

## Implementation Focus
1. Add rewiring as a first-class electrical subtrade.
2. Add repair vs new installation selector.
3. Add fault photo capture and load assessment fields.
4. Add certification badge and safety notice.

## Acceptance Notes
- Rewiring should be easier to find than generic electrical.
- The UI should warn users when a job may require a licensed electrician.
