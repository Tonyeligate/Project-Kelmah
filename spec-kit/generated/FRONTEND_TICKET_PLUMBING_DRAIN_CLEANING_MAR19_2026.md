# Ticket - Plumbing Drain Cleaning

**Date**: March 19, 2026

## Summary
Make drain cleaning a clearly tagged, easy-to-find plumbing service with strong mobile booking support.

## Job Tags
- Jobs: `Jobs`, `Search`, `Quick Jobs`, `Hirer Dashboard`
- Skill Tags: `Plumbing`, `Drain Cleaning`, `Sanitation`, `Maintenance`

## Product Gaps
- Drain cleaning is not surfaced as a distinct microservice request.
- Users need blockage severity and parts/tools context.
- Recurring drain maintenance is not clearly supported.

## Implementation Focus
1. Add drain cleaning as a separate microtrade label and search synonym.
2. Add severity selector for slow drain vs blocked drain vs sewer backup.
3. Add recurring maintenance option for households and businesses.
4. Show estimated response time and equipment needs.

## Acceptance Notes
- Drain cleaning should work as a simple one-tap quick job.
- The UI should explain if the job is urgent, recurring, or one-off.
