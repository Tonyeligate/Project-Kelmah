# Ticket - HVAC Refrigeration Repair

**Date**: March 19, 2026

## Summary
Make refrigeration repair a dedicated HVAC ticket for domestic and commercial cold systems.

## Job Tags
- Jobs: `Quick Jobs`, `Jobs`, `Search`, `Worker Search`
- Skill Tags: `HVAC`, `Refrigeration`, `Cold Room`, `Repair`

## Product Gaps
- Refrigeration is hidden inside the broad HVAC category.
- Cold room and commercial use-cases are not clearly tagged.
- Fault symptoms and parts needs are not easy to describe.

## Implementation Focus
1. Add refrigeration repair as a separate search label.
2. Add cold room and freezer type fields.
3. Add fault symptoms and parts checklist fields.
4. Add emergency downtime badge for businesses.

## Acceptance Notes
- Refrigeration repair should be discoverable for both homes and businesses.
- The form should clarify whether the job is install, repair, or maintenance.
