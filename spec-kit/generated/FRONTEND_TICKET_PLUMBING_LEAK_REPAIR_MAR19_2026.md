# Ticket - Plumbing Leak Repair

**Date**: March 19, 2026

## Summary
Turn leak repair into a first-class searchable and bookable job type for plumbing workers and hirers.

## Job Tags
- Jobs: `Jobs`, `Quick Jobs`, `Job Posting`, `Search`
- Skill Tags: `Plumbing`, `Leak Repair`, `Pipe Repair`, `Emergency`

## Product Gaps
- No dedicated leak-repair title or badge.
- Urgency and emergency pricing are not prominent enough.
- Users need photo evidence and failed-part capture.

## Implementation Focus
1. Add leak repair as a top-level job label in search and category surfaces.
2. Add urgent/emergency visibility in job cards and quick-hire flows.
3. Add photo upload and failed-part evidence fields in plumbing requests.
4. Add plumber verification and warranty note display.

## Acceptance Notes
- Leak repair must be discoverable from home, search, and quick jobs.
- Mobile users should be able to book it with minimal typing.
