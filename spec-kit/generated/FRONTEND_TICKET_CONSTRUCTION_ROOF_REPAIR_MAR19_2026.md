# Ticket - Roof Repair

**Date**: March 19, 2026

## Summary
Make roof repair a distinct high-priority construction ticket with urgent safety and weather tags.

## Job Tags
- Jobs: `Quick Jobs`, `Jobs`, `Search`, `Hirer Dashboard`
- Skill Tags: `Roofing`, `Roof Repair`, `Waterproofing`, `Construction`

## Product Gaps
- Roof repair is not exposed as a fast emergency request.
- Weather damage and leak severity are not clearly tagged.
- Users need material and roof type details.

## Implementation Focus
1. Add roof repair as a top-level construction ticket.
2. Add leak severity and weather-damage tags.
3. Add roof type, material, and slope fields.
4. Add emergency contact and response-time display.

## Acceptance Notes
- Roof repair should be easy to access during rainy-season emergencies.
- The request flow should make urgency and safety explicit.
