# Top 5 UX Microcopy

This bundle keeps the same keys across two locale variants:
- `en-GH`: Ghanaian English
- `tw`: Twi starter draft

## Locale: en-GH

### Mobile Bottom Tab Bar
- Primary CTA: Post a job
- Messages label: Messages
- Profile label: Profile
- Explore label: Browse
- Unread badge aria: You have {n} unread messages

### Fast Onboarding (Hirer)
- Title: Get started as a hirer
- Subtitle: Post your first job in under 60 seconds
- Primary CTA: Post a job now
- Secondary CTA: Skip and browse
- Helper: We'll save your draft. You can finish later.

### Job Posting Quick Wizard
- Step 1 Title: What's the job?
- Step 1 Hint: Use simple words, for example: "Paint 3 bedrooms"
- Quick Post CTA: Quick hire
- Full Post CTA: Continue to details
- Draft saved notice: Draft saved locally

### Book Now flow
- Booking CTA: Book now
- Confirmation title: Booking request sent
- Confirmation body: We've notified nearby workers. Expect responses within 30 minutes.
- Phone confirmation SMS: Your booking {JOB_ID} is received. A worker will contact you shortly.

### Mobile Money Payments
- Add mobile money CTA: Add mobile money
- Helper MTN: Use the phone number registered with MTN Mobile Money. You will receive an OTP to confirm.
- Helper Vodafone: Use the phone number registered with Vodafone Cash. Ensure you have enabled PIN transactions.
- Payment success: Payment received. Receipt sent to your phone.

## Locale: tw

### Mobile Bottom Tab Bar
- Primary CTA: Fa adwuma no to hɔ
- Messages label: Nkrasɛm
- Profile label: Wo profile
- Explore label: Hwehwɛ
- Unread badge aria: Wo nkrasɛm a wonkan da da: {n}

### Fast Onboarding (Hirer)
- Title: Hyɛ ase sɛ hirer
- Subtitle: To wo adwuma a edi kan no hɔ wɔ simma 1 mu
- Primary CTA: Fa adwuma no to hɔ seisei
- Secondary CTA: Gyae na hwehwɛ
- Helper: Yebekora wo draft no so. Wutumi wie akyiri.

### Job Posting Quick Wizard
- Step 1 Title: Dɛn na adwuma no yɛ?
- Step 1 Hint: Fa kasafua a ennyɛ den di dwuma, nhwɛso: "Paint 3 bedrooms"
- Quick Post CTA: Ntɛm to hɔ
- Full Post CTA: Kɔ so kɔ nsɛm no so
- Draft saved notice: Draft no akora so lokolyi

### Book Now flow
- Booking CTA: Book no seisei
- Confirmation title: Booking request no asɔ
- Confirmation body: Yɛabɔ adwumayɛfo a wɔbɛn wo no amanneɛ. Wubetumi anya mmuae wɔ simma 30 mu.
- Phone confirmation SMS: Wo booking {JOB_ID} no asɔ. Adwumayɛfo no bɛfrɛ wo ntɛm.

### Mobile Money Payments
- Add mobile money CTA: Fa mobile money ka ho
- Helper MTN: Fa telefon nɔma a wɔakyerɛw no MTN Mobile Money mu no di dwuma. Wubenya OTP de asi so.
- Helper Vodafone: Fa telefon nɔma a wɔakyerɛw no Vodafone Cash mu no di dwuma. Hwɛ sɛ wo PIN transaction no atu.
- Payment success: Sika no aba. Receipt no akɔ wo telefon no so.
