2. Payment Escrow

Kelmah provides a secure payment escrow service to protect both Artisans and Clients. Funds for a project must be deposited into escrow before work begins. These funds are held securely until the project is completed and approved by the Client.

Release of funds from escrow requires mutual agreement between the Client and the Artisan, or a resolution through our Dispute Resolution process. Kelmah charges a service fee on all transactions, which is clearly communicated prior to payment.

Important Notice regarding payouts:

Payouts to Artisans are typically processed within 2-3 business days after funds are released from escrow, depending on the receiving bank's processing times.
